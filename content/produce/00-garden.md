The cookbook is built around what we grow at home. When generating recipes, lean on these ingredients where appropriate and in season.

## What we grow

### Citrus & fruit
- Lemon — crops nearly year-round, sweetest in winter
- Cara Cara navel orange — pink-fleshed, sweet and low in acid; peaks in winter (roughly June–September)
- White grapefruit — winter into spring (roughly July–October)
- Purple figs — main crop late summer to autumn (roughly February–April)
- Mango — December to February, main flush January

### Herbs
- Hardy, year-round: thyme, sage, rosemary, oregano
- Summer (Nov–Mar): basil
- Cooler months (they bolt in Perth summer heat): parsley, dill

### Olives
- Home-grown and home-cured in brine; harvested late May–June, cured over the following weeks

### Nuts
- Macadamia — nuts fall March–August, main flush in autumn; husk and dry before cracking

### Occasional
- Oyster mushrooms — when we have a flush
