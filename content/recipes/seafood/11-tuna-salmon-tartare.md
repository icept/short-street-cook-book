---
title: Ginger Sesame Tuna Tartare
serves: 2
time: 15 minutes
---

The individually wrapped sashimi-grade portions from Farmer Jack's make this a proper freezer standby — pull one out in the morning and it is ready by dinner.

## Ingredients

### The fish

- about 400 g sashimi-grade tuna

### The dressing

- 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
- 1 tsp grated garlic
- 1½ tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
- 2 tbsp spring onions, finely sliced
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp tahini

### The avocado base

- 2 avocados
- 2 tsp lime juice
- 3 tbsp spring onions, finely sliced
- 1 tbsp sesame oil

## Method

1. Thaw the fish fully if frozen, then dice into 0.5 cm cubes with a sharp knife.
2. Combine the fish with the ginger, garlic, soy sauce, lime juice, sweet chilli sauce, spring onions, sesame oil, and tahini. Fold gently until evenly coated, then rest in the fridge for a few minutes while you prepare the avocado.
3. Dice or lightly mash the avocado and toss with the lime juice, spring onions, and sesame oil. Keep it slightly chunky.
4. Spoon the avocado into bowls or a plating ring, then pile the dressed fish on top.

## Substitutions

**Tuna** — salmon is an acceptable substitute; the flavour is richer and fattier but the dressing handles it equally well.
