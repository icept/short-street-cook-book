---
title: The Japanese Cocktail
serves: 1
time: 5 minutes
---

Jerry Thomas's 1862 original used almond orgeat. The macadamia version is quieter, less overtly sweet, and lets the cognac carry more of the weight — which is exactly where the weight belongs. One of the better arguments for keeping a bottle of orgeat on hand.

## Ingredients

- 60 ml cognac or high-quality brandy
- 15 ml Rich Toasted Macadamia Orgeat
- 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
- Lemon peel, to garnish

## Method

1. Combine the cognac, macadamia orgeat, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice.
2. Stir until well chilled and properly diluted.
3. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora or coupe glass.
4. Express the oils of the lemon peel over the drink and drop it in.
