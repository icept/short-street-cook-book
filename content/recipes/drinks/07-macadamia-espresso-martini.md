---
title: The Mac Espresso Martini
serves: 1
time: 5 minutes
---

The [macadamia orgeat](/recipes/syrups-cordials/05-rich-toasted-macadamia-orgeat/) replaces the usual simple syrup and adds a toasted, buttery note that works surprisingly well alongside coffee. Shake it hard — the foam is half the point.

## Ingredients

- 45 ml vodka
- 30 ml fresh espresso, hot or cold
- 15 ml coffee liqueur (Kahlúa or Mr Black)
- 15 ml [Rich Toasted Macadamia Orgeat](/recipes/syrups-cordials/05-rich-toasted-macadamia-orgeat/)
- 3 coffee beans, to garnish

## Method

1. Combine the vodka, espresso, coffee liqueur, and macadamia orgeat in a shaker with plenty of ice.
2. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds to build a thick, velvety foam.
3. Double-strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
4. Garnish with three coffee beans on top of the foam.
