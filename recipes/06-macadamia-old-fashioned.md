---
title: The Mac Old Fashioned
serves: 1
time: 5 minutes
---

The house macadamia orgeat does the job of both sugar and character here — it rounds out the whiskey with a quiet, buttery richness that a plain sugar cube never quite achieves. A drink that tastes like it has a reason to exist.

## Ingredients

- 60 ml bourbon or rye whiskey
- 7.5 ml Rich Toasted Macadamia Orgeat
- 2–3 dashes Angostura bitters
- Orange peel, to garnish

## Method

1. Combine the whiskey, macadamia orgeat, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice.
2. Stir until well chilled and properly diluted, about 20–30 seconds.
3. Strain into a rocks glass over one large ice cube.
4. Express the oils of the orange peel over the drink and drop it in.
