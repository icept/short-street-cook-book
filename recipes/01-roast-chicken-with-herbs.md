---
title: Roast Chicken with Herbs and Lemon
serves: 4
time: 1 hour 30 minutes
---

This is the chicken I make when I want a proper dinner without too much fuss.
The herbs come from the garden, the lemon from the tree at the side of the
house, and the result never feels like it takes any effort.

## Ingredients

- 1 whole chicken, about 1.6 kg
- 1 lemon, halved
- 6 sprigs fresh rosemary
- 4 sprigs fresh thyme
- 4 cloves garlic, lightly crushed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp flaked sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 200°C (fan). Pat the chicken dry inside and out with
   kitchen paper.
2. Push the lemon halves, rosemary, thyme, and garlic into the cavity.
3. Rub the bird all over with olive oil, then scatter the salt and a generous
   grind of pepper over the skin.
4. Sit the chicken breast-side up in a roasting tin and roast for 1 hour
   15 minutes, or until the juices run clear when you pierce the thigh.
5. Rest for 10 minutes before carving.
