---
title: The Macadamia Mai Tai
serves: 1
time: 5 minutes
---

Trader Vic's original called for almond orgeat; this version swaps in the house macadamia orgeat, which carries a similar richness but a distinctly local character. Use a good aged Jamaican rum — the funk holds up where something lighter would disappear.

## Ingredients

- 60 ml aged Jamaican rum (or a blend of Tiki rums)
- 22 ml fresh lime juice
- 15 ml orange curaçao (Pierre Ferrand or similar)
- 15 ml Rich Toasted Macadamia Orgeat
- Fresh mint sprig, to garnish

## Method

1. Combine the rum, lime juice, orange curaçao, and macadamia orgeat in a shaker.
2. Add crushed ice and shake briefly but vigorously to chill.
3. Pour the entire contents, ice included, into a double rocks glass. Add more crushed ice to fill if needed.
4. Garnish with a large, slapped sprig of fresh mint.
